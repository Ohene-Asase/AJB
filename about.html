About Us

Our Mission

AJ Charity foundation is a means to an end and a giveback movement that seeks to support the less privileged children in Ghana. AJ Charity foundation in its own capacity has been assisting the needy on the streets of Accra and is seeking the support of everyone to raise funds for the needs of the children , give them a shot at a better future and help them become who they are meant to be. These funds will be used to get food items, clothes, backpacks, pens and other necessary amenities of life for these promising kids. These funds would also be used to build a place for them to lay their heads, build classrooms and get them good drinking water by getting them bore holes. In charity there is no excess and a wise saying goes “we make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give”. Helping Each Other, We Can Become The People We Are Meant To Be.
